A tribute to Phil Esposito

Here's a time line of Phil Esposito's life:

  • Born: February 20, 1942 in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario
  • 1963: Phil Esposito begins his NHL career with the Chicago Blackhawks
  • 1970: wins his first Stanley Cup with Bobby Orr
  • 1972: wins his second Stanley Cup with Bobby Orr
  • 1975: Esposito plays his first game for the New York Rangers
  • 1981: Phil Esposito ends his career
  • Dec, 13th, 1987: The number 7 is retired for all Boston bruins players and Ray Bourque wears number 77.

Play with passion and heart. If you don't carry passion into sport—or in any job for that matter—you won't succeed.
